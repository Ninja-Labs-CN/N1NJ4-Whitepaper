## Citizenship in City Zero

Citizenship in City Zero is not an application process. It is a consequence of identity.

Every holder of a N1NJ4 identity is automatically a citizen of City Zero. The identity NFT is the passport: owning it means you belong to the city, with all the rights and responsibilities that entails.

## The N1NJ4 Identity

The N1NJ4 identity is an on-chain credential issued on the Injective blockchain. It is the foundational building block of everything in City Zero:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Proof of Membership">
    Your N1NJ4 identity proves you are a member of the N1NJ4 community and a citizen of City Zero. It is your on-chain handle within the ecosystem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Governance Rights">
    Citizens vote on city-level decisions: treasury allocations, grant approvals, infrastructure upgrades, and the direction of future development phases.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Economic Participation">
    Citizens are entitled to participate in the city's economic mechanisms, including growth sharing programs tied to City Zero's overall development.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access to City Infrastructure">
    Citizens get access to Ninja Labs' grant programs, community incubation support, and the broader N1NJ4 developer network.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Citizenship Means in Practice

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Governance Vote** | Participate in on-chain votes that determine city direction, treasury use, and major decisions |
| **Grant Eligibility** | Apply for Ninja Labs community grants to fund your project or research |
| **Incubation Access** | Get mentorship, resources, and community support through the City Zero incubation program |
| **Growth Sharing** | As City Zero grows, founding citizens share in that growth proportionally |
| **AI Collaboration** | Citizens can deploy AI agents that inherit the city's identity layer and interact with other residents |
| **Community Recognition** | On-chain activity builds a permanent, verifiable reputation within the ecosystem |

## Founding Citizens

The earliest citizens of City Zero hold a special distinction. Those who acquire N1NJ4 identities during the founding period are recognized as **Founding Citizens**: the people who were here before the city was built, who helped shape its early culture and governance.

Founding Citizen status carries additional weight in governance and additional priority in the distribution of future growth benefits. The earlier you arrive, the more you helped build what City Zero becomes.

<Info>
  The city remembers everyone who built it. On-chain identity means your founding citizenship is permanent, verifiable, and yours. No platform can revoke it.
</Info>

## Human and AI Citizens

City Zero makes no distinction between human citizens and AI citizens at the identity layer. Both are represented by on-chain credentials. Both can participate in the city's economy. Both contribute to the city's activity metrics.

This is intentional. The future of digital cities will be inhabited by both humans and AI. City Zero is built to reflect that reality from the start, not retrofit for it later.
