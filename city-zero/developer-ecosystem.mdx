## The Developer Ecosystem

City Zero is fundamentally a **developer city**. N1NJ4 is a developer brand. Ninja Labs is a builder collective. The entire architecture of City Zero is designed to make it as easy as possible for developers to come in, build something real, and own what they create.

The core proposition is simple: you don't build for City Zero, you build in City Zero, and what you build is yours.

## How Developers Participate

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Community Grants">
    Ninja Labs maintains a community grant program funded by the City Zero treasury. Developers can apply for grants to fund open-source infrastructure used by the ecosystem, consumer applications that serve City Zero citizens, research and tooling that advances the AI and blockchain stack, and community events, content, and education initiatives.

    Grants are approved by community governance. Citizens vote on allocations, not a central committee.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Incubation Program">
    Beyond one-time grants, City Zero offers ongoing incubation support for developers who want to build and launch their own brand or project within the ecosystem. This includes technical support from Ninja Labs engineers, community distribution through the N1NJ4 network, mentorship from experienced builders, on-chain credentialing of your project's activity, and co-building opportunities with the broader City Zero community.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Independent Brand Creation">
    City Zero explicitly supports and encourages developers to create their own independent brands within the ecosystem. You don't need to build under Ninja Labs. You can establish your own identity, your own community, and your own economic model, while remaining a citizen and contributor to the broader city.

    This is the key difference from traditional accelerators: City Zero wants you to succeed independently, not stay dependent on the city's infrastructure forever.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## The Builder Stack

City Zero provides access to a composable set of infrastructure primitives that developers can build on:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│              Your Application                    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│         City Zero Developer APIs                 │
│    (identity, governance, treasury, activity)   │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│           N1NJ4 Identity Layer                   │
│     (citizenship, credentials, reputation)      │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│            Injective Blockchain                  │
│     (EVM, high-throughput, low-fee)             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Developers inherit the full stack from day one. You don't need to build identity from scratch. You don't need to build governance from scratch. You build your product on top of a city that already has residents, infrastructure, and economic activity.

## Growth Sharing for Builders

Every developer who contributes to building City Zero, whether through a grant-funded project, an incubated brand, or independent construction, earns a proportional stake in the city's future growth.

The mechanics are on-chain and transparent. Contribution is recorded on Injective, growth sharing is calculated based on verified contribution history, and distribution happens through the City Zero treasury governance process.

<Warning>
  Growth sharing is tied to verifiable on-chain contribution. Activity that cannot be verified on-chain does not count toward growth sharing calculations. This ensures the system is fair and manipulation-resistant.
</Warning>
