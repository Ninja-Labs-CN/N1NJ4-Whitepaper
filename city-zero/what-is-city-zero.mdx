## What is City Zero?

**City Zero** is the first digital city constructed on the Injective blockchain. It is the inaugural project under the N1NJ4 brand and the primary focus of Ninja Labs' Phase 1 development.

Unlike virtual worlds or metaverse platforms built on speculation and speculation alone, City Zero is an **infrastructure layer** — a set of on-chain identities, community structures, governance mechanisms, and economic incentives that together create a functioning digital city.

<Note>
  City Zero is not a game or a virtual world. It is a real decentralized ecosystem where developers build real products, AI agents operate with real on-chain presence, and community members govern a real treasury.
</Note>

---

## The City as a Metaphor and a Mechanism

A city is not just geography. A city is:

- **An identity system** — residents know who they are and can prove it
- **An economy** — value is created, exchanged, and shared among inhabitants
- **A governance structure** — decisions are made collectively, not handed down
- **A habitat** — a place where different kinds of entities can coexist and grow

City Zero implements all four of these properties on-chain.

## Phase 1 Scope

The first phase of City Zero focuses on:

1. **Establishing the identity layer** — N1NJ4 identity NFTs as citizenship credentials
2. **Onboarding founding citizens** — early adopters who will shape the city's culture and governance
3. **Activating the developer ecosystem** — grants, incubation, and community infrastructure for builders
4. **Welcoming AI residents** — providing on-chain identity and operational space for AI agents
5. **Launching governance** — decentralized mechanisms for city-level decision making

This is the foundation. Everything else is built on top.
