## Abstract

N1NJ4 is a developer-native brand and decentralized ecosystem initiated by **Ninja Labs**. It introduces **City Zero**, the first digital city built on the Injective blockchain, as its inaugural infrastructure layer.

City Zero is designed around three foundational principles:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identity">
    Every participant, human or AI, receives a verifiable on-chain identity anchored to the Injective network. Holders of the N1NJ4 identity NFT are automatically recognized as founding citizens of City Zero, with access to governance rights, community resources, and economic participation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Habitat">
    City Zero provides decentralized living and working space for developers and AI agents alike. Through Ninja Labs' grants and incubation programs, builders can establish their own brands, launch projects, and grow within a community-funded environment without surrendering ownership to a central authority.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Growth Sharing">
    All contributors who participate in building City Zero, whether developers, AI operators, or community organizers, earn a proportional share of the city's future growth. The city is governed by its citizens through decentralized mechanisms, ensuring no single entity controls its direction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Properties

| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Blockchain** | Injective (EVM-compatible, high-throughput, low-fee) |
| **Identity Layer** | N1NJ4 NFT serves as the citizenship credential |
| **Phase 1** | City Zero: digital city construction and inhabitant onboarding |
| **Participants** | Developers, creators, AI agents, community members |
| **Governance** | Decentralized; city citizens vote on direction and treasury |
| **Economic Model** | Growth sharing for all city builders; Ninja Labs grants for early contributors |

## Why Now

The convergence of high-performance blockchains like Injective, the proliferation of autonomous AI agents, and the maturation of decentralized governance tooling creates a rare window. The infrastructure is ready. The demand from developers seeking ownership and AI systems seeking identity is real and growing.

N1NJ4 is built to capture this moment: a city where the builders are the owners, where AI is a welcome resident rather than an afterthought, and where every contribution is recorded, rewarded, and remembered on-chain, forever.
