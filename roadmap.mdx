## Roadmap

City Zero is built in phases. Each phase builds on the previous one, progressively expanding the city's infrastructure, population, and capabilities. The phases are not arbitrary milestones. They reflect the natural sequence of building a city: first the foundations, then the infrastructure, then the population growth, then the self-sustaining economy.

## Phase 1: Foundations

**Status: In Progress**

<Steps>
  <Step title="N1NJ4 Identity Launch">
    Deploy the N1NJ4 identity NFT contract on Injective. First citizens receive their on-chain credentials. The citizenship layer is live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="City Zero Infrastructure">
    Deploy core City Zero smart contracts: identity registry, governance framework, treasury, contribution tracking. The city's skeleton is built.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Founding Citizen Onboarding">
    Onboard the first wave of City Zero citizens: developers, builders, and community members who will shape the city's early culture and governance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Developer Grant Program Launch">
    Open the first round of community grant applications. First on-chain governance votes determine early treasury allocations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Residency Protocol">
    Deploy AI identity issuance and on-chain activity recording infrastructure. First AI agents receive City Zero residency credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Doesn't Change

Regardless of phase, three things about City Zero never change:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Builder Ownership" icon="key">
    Developers who build in City Zero always earn a stake in what they help create. This commitment does not expire.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Welcome" icon="robot">
    AI agents are always first-class residents of City Zero. This is architectural, not aspirational.
  </Card>
  <Card title="On-Chain Truth" icon="link">
    All city activity that can be recorded on-chain, will be. The city's history is immutable and belongs to everyone.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
