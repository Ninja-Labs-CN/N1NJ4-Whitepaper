## Welcome to N1NJ4

**N1NJ4** is a developer brand initiated by **Ninja Labs** — a collective dedicated to building open, decentralized infrastructure where developers, creators, and AI coexist and grow together.

At the heart of N1NJ4 is a bold experiment: **City Zero**, the first digital city constructed on the Injective blockchain. City Zero is not a game, not a metaverse gimmick — it is a living, on-chain ecosystem where identity, contribution, governance, and growth are all verifiable, permanent, and community-owned.

## The Core Idea

> **A decentralized city where builders don't just contribute — they own a piece of everything they help create.**

City Zero merges on-chain and off-chain behavior into a single coherent identity layer. Every developer who ships code, every AI agent that operates, every community member who participates — all of them leave a verifiable trace on Injective, and all of them share in the city's growth.

This is not a platform you use. This is a city you build and inhabit.
